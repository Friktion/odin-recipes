Lasagna

A Fantastic Homemade Lasagna Meal

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Noodles
  • Mozzarella
Steps
  1. Cook Ground Beef
  2. Sautee Onions and Garlic
  3. Prepare your sauce
  4. Boil your noodles
  5. Lay out first layer of noodles onto a baking sheet
  6. Combine and mix cooked ingredients into a bowl.
  7. Using a spoon, scoop cooked ingredients onto the layer of noodles
  8. Add cheese on top of cooked ingredients
  9. Add another layer of noodles
  10. Repeat steps 7-9 until you have reached desired lasagna height
  11. Top with Mozarella cheese and sauce; bake at 375F and 25 minutes
